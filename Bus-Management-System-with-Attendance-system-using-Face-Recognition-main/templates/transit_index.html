Abstract

The Smart Campus Transit System with Online Bus Pass, Face-Based Attendance & Live Tracking is an integrated web and Android-based application designed to automate and secure college transportation services. The system replaces the traditional paper-based bus pass mechanism with a digital platform that enables students to apply and renew bus passes online. It uses face recognition and QR verification to authenticate students during boarding and drop-off, ensuring safety and preventing misuse.

The system sends real-time notifications to parents when students board the bus and when they safely arrive at the destination. Additionally, GPS-based live bus tracking allows parents and administrators to monitor bus locations in real time. This solution enhances operational efficiency, improves student safety, ensures transparency, and reduces administrative workload.
